Materials

You can find tables and raw-data about all metals and armor-materials in the mod on the wiki:

http://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Masterwork:Metal

The Hermit has access to any material that can be produced in tanner and smelter, but no alloys like bronze or steel. Upgraded leathers, bone, smelted ores as well as a few selected rock, wood and gem weapons and armors. You may salvage some higher-tier materials from invaders.

There are no unique materials that are new to hermit mode.
