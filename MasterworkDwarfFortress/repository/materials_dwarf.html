Materials

You can find tables and raw-data about all metals and armor-materials in the mod on the wiki:

http://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Masterwork:Metal

Dwarves have access to several new metals and leathers that are pretty unique. Master metalsmiths and forgers, they can work adamantine and make clothing from gold, silver or mithril strands.

Moonsilver is blessed silver from the Oracle of Armok. It requires magma to be made. It does extreme damage against all things evil.

Shadesilver is blessed silver from the Oracle of Armok. It requires magma to be made. It does extreme damage against all things good.

Volcanic is made in the volcanic foundry. Its better than steel and does extra damage against frost giants, but is very expensive and requires magma.

Bifrost is half as good as adamantine, which makes it way better than steel. It can only be gained by killing frost giant invaders.

Dragonscales are steel-grade and temperature immune, you can only get them by killing dragons, or by luck, if your archeologist finds them.

Netherbark is steel-grade and eternally cold leather, made from nethercap, a tree that grows in the third cavern. You need a herblists lab for process these.
