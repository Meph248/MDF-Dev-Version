Items

Weapons

Short sword, spear, axe, hammer and bow. These are your basic weapons. You can also makes daggers from bone or horn. Training swords, training spears, training axes and training hammers can be used in the training dummy to raise your skills.

Chopping axes can cut down trees and picks are used to mine. You can also make breakable shovels from wood. They act like a pick, but will fall apart after a week.

Ammo

Arrows for your bow, made as always in the craftsman or the forge. You can also make rock and gem arrows on workbenches. You can poison arrows, or make flaming/exploding arrows with the help of the Alchemy workbench and a brazier to light them.

Armor

You can make leather armors, as well as all normal metal armors. Boots, Greaves, Breastplates, Helmets and Gauntlets.

Shield

Bucklers are your shield of choice. Can be made of wood or any metal.

Clothing

The leatherworker and clothier can not make clothing. You have to make a Workbench (Cloth) to get clothing of wool, cotton or silk, or a Workbench (Leather) to get leather, fur, scale, chitin or shell clothing.

Tools

All vanilla tools are available, including mortar and pestle, used to make alchemy and poison workbenches.

Trapcomps

You have access to spikes, sawblades, axeblades, pump mechanisms and razorwire. They are made in the usual places, like carpenter and forge. A few are available as rock and gem version on the fitting workbenches.

Special

After 3 years in the game, your hermit will stumble over a strange platinum talisman. See the building section and the magic altar for more info. This item is so valuable that it will bring your wealth up high enough to cause sieges.
