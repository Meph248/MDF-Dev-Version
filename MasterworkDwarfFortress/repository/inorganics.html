Chrome, Cobalt, Mithril, Titanium and Wolfram also have a patternwelded variant. You can produce those in the Metallurgist. They are similar to the original metals, but with increased stats. Cobalt gets close to steel, Mithril even sharper then steel and Wolfram even better for blunt weapons. Chrome and Titanium get more valuable.

Silver, Gold, Orichalcum and Adamantine can all be used for clothing.
