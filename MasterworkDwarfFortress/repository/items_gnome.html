A few words about crafting:

Gnomes are excellent artificers. Many of their armor and weapons have special properties. To create their high-level items, you'll need a Metalsmith's Forge, a Tinker's Workshop, and a Gadgeteer's Workshop: the Metalsmith produces the framework of the item, the Tinker produces the special parts, and the Gadgeteer combines the two to create useful equipment. Item crafting can take a while, but once you get the hang of it you'll be able to create useful, high-tech equipment with surprisingly few raw materials.

Shields

Gnome 'shields' often have special abilities. Most of them are more like secondary ranged weapons that can be used to complement a regular ranged weapon like a pistol or a pistol crossbow, or to give extra power to a melee fighter. They can be produced at the Gadgeteer's Workshop by combining a regular arm-guard with devices produced in the Tinker's Workshop.

Name Items to produce Special Effects
Smart Guard Arm Guard, Clockwork Device, Brain, Actuator, Lens High blocking chance
Thunder Caster Arm Guard, Power Cell, Transformer, Capacitor, Key Paralyzing ranged electric attack
Ion Caster Arm Guard, Cathode Ray Tube, Electromagnet, Key Damaging particle beam attack
Flame Caster Arm Guard, Glass Tube, Power Cell, Electromagnet, Key, Blood of Armok Ranged fireball attack
Frost Caster Arm Guard, Glass Tube, Power Cell, Electromagnet, Key, Tear of Armok Ranged ice web attack
Dark Matter Caster Arm Guard, Glass Tube, Power Cell, Electromagnet, Key, Bone of Armok Ranged slade glob attack
Warpstone Caster Arm Guard, Glass Tube, Power Cell, Electromagnet, Key, Stable Warpstone Ranged warpstone radiation attack

Gnome armor is not especially powerful, but it can have interesting effects. To produce their special armor types, start with a 'gear' armor type produced in the Metalsmith's Forge, then convert it into something at the Gadgeteer's Workshop by combining it with items produced in the Tinker's Workshop.

Another kind of armor, 'adjustable' armor, can be applied to trained animals at an Animal Care Area.
