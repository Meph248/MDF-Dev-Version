Items

Kobolds are too small to use most weapons. Not a single hammer, pike or crossbow can be wielded by the average bold. Most weapons invaders and caravans bring have to be melted down and remade into kobold weapons. Besides their smaller and weaker weapons, you also have smaller and weaker armors, but very decent bolas and AoE totem bombs for traps.

Weapons

Picks (Mining) ::: Kobolds have to steal picks from dwarves or drow first.

Chopper (Axe) ::: A small axe to chop wood.

Pointy Stick (Spear) ::: A small sharp weapon that does piercing damage.

Machete (Sword) ::: A small cutting weapon.

Club (Mace) ::: A blunt weapon. Good against armor.

Flail (Whip) ::: A blunt lashing weapon.

Scourge (Whip) ::: An edged lashing weapon.

 

Ranged Weapons

Sling (Sling-Skill) ::: Shoots sling bullets. Standart ranged weapon.

Blowgun (Blowgun) ::: Shoots darts. Can use poisons.

Bola Thrower (Bola-Skill) ::: Shoots bolas. Large cutting damage.

Bow (Bow) ::: Shoots arrows. Piercing/Cutting/Blunt damage. Foreign.

Totem-Sling (Bombardier) ::: Shoots totem-bombs. Special AoE effect.

Please note that all ammo types still need quivers. Even Throwing weapons or bullets.

 

TrapComps

Made in Trap Setter:

Shrapnel trap ::: Needs bone/wood to be made. 4 hits, edged.

Trapdarts ::: Need bone/wood to be made, very cheap. 1 weak hit, edged.

Sharp Whiplash ::: Needs leather/thread, 1 hit, edged.

Knotted Whiplash ::: Needs leather/thread, 3hits, blunt.

Totemhammer ::: Needs totem/wood, 1 heavy hit, blunt.

Axe Blade ::: 1 hit, edged. Upgrades give more attacks.

Saw Blade ::: 1 hit, edged. Upgrades cut deeper and add one more attack.

Corkscrew ::: 1 hit, very very bad. Used in pumps.

Spike ::: 1 hit, used for upright spike traps. Upgrade cuts deeper or larger wounds.

Made in Bombardier:

Booby-trap ::: 1 hit, uses ammo. Totem-bombs that are made in batches of three. Has AoE effect on target. Use with care!

 

Shields

Blocking bracer ::: Blockchance 15%

 

Armors

Necklace ::: Normal shirt, soft

Vest ::: Normal shirt, soft

Cloak ::: Normal shirt, soft. Can be worn over armor.

Suit of Armor ::: Armored, shaped. (like leather armor)

Plated Hide ::: Armored, shaped. (like breastplates)

 

Pants

Banded skirts ::: Normal pants, soft.

Loincloth ::: Normal pants, soft.

Thight-Guard ::: Armored pants, shaped. (like greaves)

 

Gloves

Wrist-Guards ::: Armored gloves, shaped. (like gauntlets)

 

Shoes

Ankle bands ::: Normal shoes, soft.

Shin-Guards ::: Armored shoes, shaped. (Like boots)

 

Helmets

Cap ::: Armored helm, shaped.
