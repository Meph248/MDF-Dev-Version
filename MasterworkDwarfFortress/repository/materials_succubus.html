Succubus materials

Available metals

The succubi unfortunatly did not develop metallurgy as well as other races and are outclassed by dwarves. You will be unable to make steel. It is a good idea to sort the spoils from sieges and melt down steel and mithril items you will find, with perhaps the exception of breastplates that you can wear on top of corsets. You can also obtain rusty steel or iron and clean it at the metallurgist's forge.

Ironbone and bloodsteel

Building an alchemist's cauldron will allow the production of ironbone using ash and bones. It is then possible to transmute the ironbone into bloodsteel using blood.

Blood can be obtained at the kitchen or slaughterhouse.

Stygian bronze

Stygian Bronze is made at the metallurgist's forge using bronze and flux stone. In addition to yielding even more metal per reaction (2 bars for 1 unit of bronze), stygian bronze in unnaturally lightweight. This metal is good for armor, providing protection without hurting speed and is okay for cutting weapons. It is bad for blunt weapons and non bladed whips.

If you are running short of flux stone, you can grind bone at the Bonemill as a replacement.

Basiliskine

Basiliskine is, like stygian bronze, a lightweight metal suited for cutting weapons and armors. Its resistance is at the same level as steel, making it your best choice of protection without sacrificing mobility.

It is obtained by shearing lesser basilisk (or their larger cousins) to obtain a horn that can be treated at the smelter. Lesser basilisks can be obtained at the Summoning Circle using a bar of lead and a flux material.

Leather

Having leather or their equivalent will allow you to further protect your succubi. Leather armor can be worn over corsets and chain mail can be replaced by bodysuits.

Mining hazards

Warpstone, usually fatal to those who mine it, is beneficial to Succubi. Your miners get positive effects from mining both weak and unstable warpstone. Anthracite however remain hazardous to mine, unless you capture and convert dwarves. Devils are immune to black lung.

Glass mass production

After discovering magma, or spawning some at the magma well, you will unlock the floating glass furnace. This workshop will take raw glass to produce blocks and windows in great quantities.
