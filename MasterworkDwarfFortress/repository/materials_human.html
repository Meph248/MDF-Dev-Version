Materials

You can find tables and raw-data about all metals and armor-materials in the mod on the wiki:

http://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Masterwork:Metal

Humans dont have any unique materials. Its that simple. You use normal rocks, metals and gems you mine or trade for. If you are looking for info on stygian bronze you bought from Succubi or dreadnought items bought from Warlocks, please go to their material sections.
